Frequently Asked Questions

Is it free to list my sale?

Yes, 100% free. There are no fees to list your garage sale, estate sale, moving sale, or neighbourhood sale on Garage Hoppers.

How long does it take for my listing to go live?

Listings are reviewed and typically approved within a few hours. Once approved, your listing appears immediately on the site.

Can I edit my listing after submitting?

Not yet through the site, but email us at hello@garagehoppers.ca with your listing details and we'll update it for you.

What areas does Garage Hoppers cover?

We cover all of Canada. You can set your search radius up to 200 km from any city or town.

What types of sales can I list?

Garage sales, estate sales, moving sales, and neighbourhood sales. We do not list commercial sales, online-only sales, or business liquidations.

How do I get more people to my sale?

Add clear photos — listings with photos get significantly more views. Write a detailed description and list your best items. Share your Garage Hoppers listing on Facebook and Nextdoor too.

Can I list a sale for a future date?

Yes. You can submit a listing for any upcoming date. We recommend posting at least 3–5 days before your sale.

How do I contact a seller?

Seller contact information is not currently shown publicly on listing pages. The best approach is to show up during the listed hours, or post a comment on the listing if that feature is added.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

What we collect

When you submit a listing, we collect your name, email address, and the details of your sale (address, dates, description, photos). Your name and email are never shown publicly. Your sale's street address, city, and province are shown on your listing so shoppers can find you.

When you browse the site, we may collect anonymous usage data (pages visited, general location by region) through analytics tools to understand how the site is used. We do not collect personal data from browsers.

How we use it

Your contact information is used only to communicate with you about your listing — for example, to confirm approval or to let you know if your listing was removed. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Cookies

We use minimal cookies necessary to operate the site. If Google AdSense ads are enabled, Google may set cookies for ad personalization. You can opt out of personalized ads through Google's Ad Settings.

Data retention

Listing data is retained for as long as the listing is active. You can request deletion of your data at any time by emailing hello@garagehoppers.ca.
